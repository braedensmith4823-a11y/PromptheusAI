Frequently Asked Questions

Is it cheating to use AI?

No, AI is like having an intern, who does not get distracted. It can do tons of grunt and busy work for you, and you do not need to pay it. However, just like an intern it needs clear instructions. The more time you put into crafting your prompt, the better result you will receive.

Is AI secure and private?

Yes—when deployed correctly. It’s crucial to work with vetted tools and enforce strong data protection policies. Systems like Ask Sage have been specially developed under government contract to ensure security.

Can employees use AI without coding?

Absolutely. Many no-code tools allow users to generate reports, analyze data, or draft content using simple interfaces.
